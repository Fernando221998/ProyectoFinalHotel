Welcome to Hotel Viriato Seville

The Hotel Viriato is located in the heart of Seville, we are in Viriato street, its privileged location is ideal for visiting the main monuments of Seville such as La Giralda, Cathedral, Torre del Oro, Archivo de Indias, Reales Alcázares de Sevilla and entertainment sites in the city, you will have them a few meters away, being able to rest whenever you feel like it in your comfortable room because of the proximity we have to all the places of interest. From the Hotel Viriato it will take 10 minutes to reach the Giralda on foot.

If you are looking for a hotel to stay in the Andalusian capital with charm, maximum comfort and good services, it is the perfect place for it. And remember that by booking here on our Official Website, you will find the best price guaranteed.
